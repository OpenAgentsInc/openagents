# Product

## Register

product

## Users

OpenAgents users, operators, contributors, and public visitors who need to
inspect public-safe network evidence without internal authority, private
payloads, or settlement data.

## Product Purpose

OpenAgents.com exposes the public product surface for OpenAgents: account
entry, forum and proof surfaces, Pylon/contributor evidence, product promises,
and network statistics. Success means visitors can quickly distinguish live
public evidence from unavailable, bounded, or operator-only claims.

## Brand Personality

Operational, precise, restrained. The interface should feel like a trustworthy
command surface: compact, dark, factual, and allergic to decorative filler.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-card hero pages, bright SaaS gradients, decorative charts,
generic chat-first assistant framing, fake metrics, and copy that implies
private authority or settlement certainty where only public projection evidence
exists.

## Design Principles

- Evidence first: every visible claim should read as sourced, bounded, or
  explicitly unavailable.
- Dense but scannable: public proof pages should prioritize fast comparison
  over spectacle.
- Keep chrome quiet: navigation, labels, and helper copy should stay short so
  the data remains primary.
- Preserve authority boundaries: public UI can summarize public projections
  but must not imply payout, settlement, or runtime authority it does not own.
- Dark operational continuity: new surfaces should inherit the Vortex-style
  mono, black-panel, subtle-border vocabulary from `DESIGN.md`.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Default to high-contrast text on black surfaces, keyboard-readable native links
and controls, reduced-motion-safe states, and text fallbacks for visual data
such as charts.
