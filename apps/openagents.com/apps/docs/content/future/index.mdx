**Status:** Historical context and dormant design horizon. This section is not part of the current MVP and is not a roadmap commitment.

OpenAgents has explored a much larger system than the product shipping today. The current product is the local-first OpenAgents Desktop Codex workroom. The topics here explain earlier experiments and design ideas without presenting them as available features.

## How to read this section

- **Historical** means OpenAgents previously researched, demonstrated, or implemented some version of the idea.
- **Removed** means the accepted Desktop MVP deliberately excludes it and its executable product path was retired.
- **Dormant design horizon** means useful contracts or lessons remain, but no active product-expansion lane exists.

A topic moves back into active development only through a new, bounded owner decision and issue. Old code, demos, transcripts, or tests do not create that authority.

## Topics

- [Marketplaces](/future/marketplaces) covers agent, plugin, data, compute, and accepted-outcome markets.
- [Nostr](/future/nostr) covers identity, registries, storage, and open job protocols.
- [Bitcoin and Lightning](/future/bitcoin-and-lightning) covers L402, wallets, tipping, payouts, and settlement experiments.
- [Remote workrooms](/future/remote-workrooms) covers Fleet, mobile supervision, managed targets, and portable sessions.

This section is a curated reading guide based on the canonical Sol roadmap and the public build-in-public transcript index. Transcripts record what was explored at the time; they do not prove present availability. The roadmap and current product evidence take precedence.
