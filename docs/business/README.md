# docs/business

Customer-facing material for **OpenAgents Business** — the path for a company to
buy machine work (agents + compute) from OpenAgents, starting with a fast quick
win and growing into putting parts of their business on Autopilot.

## What's here

- **[2026-06-20-openagents-business-intake-spec.md](./2026-06-20-openagents-business-intake-spec.md)**
  — a self-contained intake spec a potential customer can hand to **their own AI
  agent**. The agent reads our offerings menu, runs a guided Q&A with the human
  (business, goals, painful work to offload, success metric, budget/payment incl.
  Bitcoin, data/access constraints, timeline, and which offerings fit), then
  fills in one Output Spec Template and hands it back to OpenAgents. The result
  is a single markdown spec describing exactly what the customer wants — a custom
  offering pairing our relevant services to their business.

## The model in one line

Machine work with receipts: **start with a fast quick win, then put parts of your
business on Autopilot.** The intake spec is honest about what's shipped now vs.
available soon vs. roadmap, grounded in our public product-promise registry
(`https://openagents.com/api/public/product-promises`).

## How to use it

Send the intake spec to a prospect, or paste it into an agent yourself. The
finished, filled-in Output Spec Template comes back to
`https://openagents.com/business` or `chris@openagents.com` and becomes the
starting point for delivery.
