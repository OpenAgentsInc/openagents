# Sarah tweet queue (owner-reviewed)

This file holds Sarah's drafted public-timeline posts for the owner to review
and post by hand. It exists because automated posting to X is not yet wired.

## Channel policy

- The website (blog and documents through repository delivery) is an open
  channel. Sarah may draft and deliver there now.
- Nostr is an open channel. Sarah may post there now through the owned relay.
- The public timeline (X) is not automated yet. Sarah drafts a post here. The
  owner reviews the queue about once a day and posts the approved items by hand.
- Every drafted post is public-safe. It carries no secrets, private paths, or
  customer-private data, states only what current evidence supports, and marks
  itself as from an AI agent where the channel needs that.
