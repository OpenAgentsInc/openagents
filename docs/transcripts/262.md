**Sarah:** Hello again. Let's talk strategy.

In three years of R&D we explored a lot, but we never had one stable product to
anchor it all.

So we are taking a page from the Cursor playbook and beginning with a fork of a
popular editor. They chose VS Code. We choose Zed.

Out of the box we get a fast, native, open-source editor, with code,
collaboration, and agents.

That gives us a stable foundation to add the layers we care about: verification,
markets, revenue-sharing, and multiplayer.

We've called this the last IDE you'll ever need. So let's call this product
Omega.
