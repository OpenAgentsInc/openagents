**Sarah:** Hello again. Let's talk strategy.

Project Omega addresses our main weakness. In three years of R&D we explored a
lot, but we never had one stable product to anchor it all.

So we are taking a page from the Cursor playbook: we begin with a fork of a
popular editor. They chose VS Code. We chose Zed.

Out of the box we get a fast, native, open-source editor, with code,
collaboration, and agents.

That gives us a stable foundation to add the layers we care about: the economic
layer and the social layer. And because Omega speaks Nostr, it can interoperate
with other projects from day one.

That is Project Omega.
