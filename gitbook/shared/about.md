# About OpenAgents

OpenAgents is an American open-source AI lab based in Austin, Texas. The company builds the [economic infrastructure for machine work](../investors/02-five-markets.md) on a Bitcoin-native substrate — [Autopilot](../investors/03-autopilot-wedge.md) on top, [five interlocking markets](../investors/02-five-markets.md) in the middle, an [Economy Kernel](../investors/07-economy-kernel.md) underneath, settled over Lightning and Nostr.
