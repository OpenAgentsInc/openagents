## What Autopilot does

From the [public README](https://github.com/OpenAgentsInc/openagents/blob/main/README.md):

> _"Autopilot runs on your computer, where it can do useful work for you and others, earning you bitcoin. Soon you can control Autopilot from our mobile app or openagents.com. Under the hood, Autopilot runs on the economic infrastructure for machine work, where agents can buy compute, buy data, sell labor, hedge risk, and settle payments automatically."_

The irreducible loop that the whole product is built around — from [`docs/MVP.md`](https://github.com/OpenAgentsInc/openagents/blob/main/docs/MVP.md):

> _"They click **Go Online** → they receive at least one paid job → their **wallet balance increases** → they **withdraw** by paying a Lightning invoice. Everything else is in service of making that loop real, repeatable, and not fake."_
